The calendar we use now.

1000ms → 1sec

60sec → 1min

60min → 1h

24h → 1 day

7 days → 1 week

28-31 days → 1 month

12 months → 1 year

But every 4 years, there’s a leap year where Feb has 1 more day. But every 100 years, there isn’t a leap year (all just to account for the Earth’s rotation).
